I've been staring at a blank page for almost an hour now.. Yes I'm one of those oldschool writers who still uses pen and paper. Anyway, it's been an hour of trying to figure out how to introduce myself, you know, how not to sound like one of those angry, toxic or attention seeking feminists; or whatever else men call us to silence , discredit and defame us.

To some extent they aren't entirely wrong: (a) I am angry, (b) depending on which of my exes you talk to, i have had toxic tendencies before (you know the kind of toxic poets rave about? Yap once upon a time that was me.take note of the past tense used) and finally, we all in one way or another suffer from ADHD(Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder) All that said, I, Bhutsiri, am a FEMINIST. .

FEMINIST: a person who believes in the social, political and economic equality of the sexes.

So who am i really;

When they ask you what type of person I am..

Tell Im a feminist.

Tell Im an African. .

Tell them I know I've got a universe between my thighs so ain't no man telling me what to do and what not to, what to where*wear and how to carry this body.

Tell them just like Miss Maya said, she dances like shes got diamond at the meeting of her thighs

When they ask, tell them Im a slave, for I can never be free until every woman knows of freedom..

When they ask, tell them, that one? She's a fighter. I've fought for my place in this world, I've fought my way out of the hands of men who thought they could break me and turn me into another femicide statistic.

And so I tell you, when they ask you what type of person I really arm*am, be sure to let them know that still, like dust, I rise.
